2 rakaat sunnah

Setelah salam,

Asyhadu an laa ilaaha illallaah, wa asyhadu anna Muhammadan `abduhu wa Rasuuluh (3 kali)

Iqaamatu ‘sh-shalaat, wa iitaa’u ‘z-zakaat, wa shawmu Ramadhaan, wa hajjul bayti Haqq. Aamantu billaahi wa malaa’ikatihi wa kutubihi wa rusulihii wa ‘l-yawmi ‘l-aakhiri, wa bi ‘l-qadri khayrihi wa syarrihi min Allaahi ta`aalaa Haqq – Qabuulin yaa Rabb.

Awda`naa haatayni ‘l-kalimatayni ‘sy-syahaadatayni `indaka yaa Sayyidi yaa Rasuul-Allaah. Wa hiiya lanaa wadii`atun yawma ‘l-qiyaamah, yaa man arsalahu ‘l-Laahu ta`aala rahmatan lil `aalamiin.

Subhaanallaah wa bihamdihi - Subhaanallaahi ‘l-`Azhiim, Astaghfirullaah (100 kali)

Astaghfirullaaha ‘l-`Azhiim min kulli dzanbin wa ma`shiyatin min kulli maa yukhaalifu diinal Islaam, min kulli maa yukhaalifu ‘sy-Syarii`ah, min kulli maa yukhaalifu ‘th-thariiqah, min kulli maa yukhaalifu ‘l-haqiiqah, min kulli maa yukhaalifu ‘l-`aziimah, min kulli maa yukhaalifu ‘l-ma`rifaat, yaa arhama ‘r-raahimin, yaa Allaah

Astaghfirullaaha ‘l-`azhiim wa atuubu ilayh (100 kali)

Astaghfirullaaha ‘l-`azhiim wa atuubu ilayh, tawbata `abdin zhaalimin li nafsihi, laa yamliku linafsihi, mawtan walaa hayaatan walaa nusyuuraa.

Allaahuma Anta Rabbii, laa ilaaha illaa Anta khalaqtanii wa anaa `abduka wa anaa `alaa `ahdika wa wa` dika maastatha`tu.

A`uudzubika min syarri maa shana`tu, wa abuu’u laka bi ni`matika `alayya, wa abuu’u bi dzanbiyy faghfirlii dzanbiyy, fa innahu laa yaghfiru ‘dz-dzunuuba illaa Anta, yaa Allaah.

A`uudzu billaahi mina ‘sy-syaythaani ‘r-rajim/Bismillaahi ‘r-Rahmaani ‘r-Rahiim/Rabbanaa laa tuzigh quluubanaa, ba`da idz hadaytanaa wa hablanaa min ladunka rahmatan, innaka anta ‘l-Wahhaab

Yaa Wahhaab, yaa Wahhaab, yaa Wahhaab, yaa Musabbiba ‘l-Asbaab, yaa Mufattiha ‘l-Abwaab, yaa Muqalliba ‘l-quluubi wa ‘l-Abshaar, yaa Daliilal mutahayyiriin, yaa Ghiyaatsal Mustaghitsiin, yaa Hayyu, ya Qayyuum, yaa Dzal Jalaali wal Ikraam, wa ufawwidhu amriiy ila ‘l-Laah, inna’l-Laaha bashiirun bi ‘l-`ibaad

Ya man laa malja’a minhu illaa ilayhi fa laa tukhayyib rajaa’ana, yaa Qadiima ‘l-Ihsaan

Allaahumma ahsin ilaynaa bi ihsaanika ‘l-qadiim, yaa Allah

Laa taqnathuu min rahmatillaah, inna ‘l-Laaha yaghfiru ‘dz-dzunuuba jamii`a, innahuu Huwwa ‘l-Ghafuuru ‘r-Rahiim

Allahumma innaa nas’aluka ‘l-`afwa wa ‘l-`aafiyata fi ‘d-diini wa ‘d-dunyaa wa ‘l-aakhirah

Allaahumma ‘s-turnaa bi satrika ‘l-jamiil

Surat al-Ikhlash (3 kali)

Subhaana Rabbika Rabbi ‘l-`izzati `ammaa yashifuun, wa salaamun `alaa ‘l-Mursaliina wa ‘l-hamdu li’l-Laahi Rabbi ‘l-`aalamiin

Laa ilaaha illa’l-Laahu, wahdahu laa syariikalah, lahu ‘l-mulku wa lahu ‘l-hamdu, yuhyii wa yumiit, wa Huwa Hayyun daa’imun, laa yamuut, bi yadihil khayr, wa Huwa `alaa kulli syay’in qadiir

Ihda:

Ilaa syarafi ‘n-Nabiyy (s) wa-aalihii wa Shahbihi ‘l-kiraam wa ilaa arwaahi ikhwaanihil mina ‘l-anbiyaa’i wa ‘l-mursaliin wa khudamaa’i syaraa `ihim wa ilaa arwaahi a’immati ‘l-arba`ah wa ilaa arwaahi masyaayikhina ‘l-kiraam fi ‘th-thariiqati ‘n-naqsyabandiyyati ‘l-aliyyah, khaashshatan ilaa ruuhi iimaami ‘th-thariiqati wa ghawtsi ‘l-khaliiqah Syaykh Bahaa’uddiin Naqsyabandi Muhammadini ‘l-Uwaysiyyi ‘l-Bukhaarii, wa Mawlana Sulthaani ‘l-Awliyaa’i Syaykh `Abdillah Faa’iz ad-Daaghistaanii, wa syaykhina ustaadzinaa Sulthaani ‘l-Awliyaa’i Syaykh Sayyid Muhammad Naazhim `Aadil Al-Qubruusi Al-Haqqaanii, wa hadhrati ustaadzinaa wa ustaadzi ustaadzinaa wa ‘sh-shiddiqiyyiin, al-Faatihah

Berbaring ke arah qiblat dari sisi kanan (seperti posisi jenazah) sambil berdoa:

Bismillaahi ‘r-rahmani ‘r-rahiim.

Qaaluu maa antum illaa basyarun mitslunaa wa maa anzala ‘r-rahmaanu min syay’in in antum illa takdzibuun

Wa maa `alaynaa illa ‘l-balaaghu ‘l-mubiin

In kaanat illaa shayhatan waahidatan fa idzaahum khaamiduun

Wa in kullun lammaa jamii`un ladaynaa muhdharuun

Wa’m-taazu ‘l-yawma ayyuha ‘l-mujrimuun (“Aadzana Allaah” )

Alam a`had ilaykum yaa banii aadama allaa ta`budu ‘sy-syaythaan

innahuu lakum `aduwwun mubiin

Wa ani`buduunii, haadzaa shiraathun mustaqiim

Wa laqad adhalla minkum jibillan katsiira, afalam takuunuu ta`qiluun

Haadzihii jahannamu ‘l-latii kuntum tuu`aduun

Ishlawha ‘l-yawma bimaa kuntum takfuruun

Alyawma nakhtimu `alaa afwaa hihim wa tukallimunaa aydiihim wa tasyhadu arjuluhum bimaa kaanuu yaksibuun

Wa law nasyaa’u lathamasnaa `alaa a`yunihim fa ‘s-tabaqu ‘sh-shiraatha fa annaa yubshiruun

Wa law nasyaa’u lamasakhnaahum `alaa makaanatihim famastathaa`uu mudhiyyan wa laa yarji`uun

Wa man nu`ammirhu nunakkis-hu fi ‘l-khalqi afalaa ya`qiluun

Wa maa `allamnaahu ‘sy-syi`ra wa maa yambaghii lahu, in huwa illaa dzikrun wa qur’aanun mubiin

Li yundzira man kaana hayyan wa yahiqqan qawlu `ala ‘l-kaafiriin

Awalam yaraw annaa khalaqnaa lahum mimma `amilat aydiina an`aaman fahum lahaa maalikuun

Wa dzallalnaahaa lahum faminhaa rakuubuhum wa minhaa ya’kuluun

Wa lahum fiiha manaafi`u masyaaribu afalaa yasykuruun

Wa ‘t-takhadzuu min duuni ’l-Laahi aalihatan laa`allahum yunsharuun

Laa yastathii`uuna nashrahum wahum lahum jundum muhdharuun

Fa laa yahzunka qawluhum, innaa na`lamu maa yusirruuna wa maa yu`linuun

Awalam yara ‘l-insaanu annaa khalaqnaahu min nuthfatin fa idzaa huwa khashiimun mubiin

Wa dharaba lanaa matsalan wa nasiya khalqahu qaala man yuhyi ‘l-`izhaama wa hiya ramiim

Qul yuhyiiha ‘l-ladzii ansya’ahaa awwala marrah, wa huwa bi kulli syai’in `aliim

Alladzii ja`ala lakum mina ‘sy-syajari ‘l-akhdhari naaran fa idzaa antum minhu tuuqiduun

Awalaysa ‘l-ladzii khalaqa ‘s-samaawaati wa ‘l-ardha bi qaadirin `alaa an yakhluqa mitslahum, balaa wa huwa ‘l-khallaaqu ‘l-`aliim

Innamaa amruhuu idzaa araada syay’an an yaquula lahuu kun fayakuun

Fa subhaana ‘l-ladzii bi yadihii malakuutu kulli syai’in wa ilayhi turja`uun. (Kullu syay’in haalikun illaa wajhahu, lahul hukmu wa ilayhi turja`uun) Amana bi ‘l-Lah shadaqa ‘l-Laahu ‘l-Mawlaana ‘l-`Azhiim
